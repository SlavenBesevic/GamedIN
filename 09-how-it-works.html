HOW IT WORKS?

Hello Gamers, are you ready to play new games for free, explore and enjoy Gamedin community. Take a look how everything works in few simple steps.

Join for free

Sign up on website and register an account with username.

Contribute a game

Go to Add Video Game page and select game or games you would like to contribute to our library. Each game has specific value - points which are based on demand and our unique formula.

Recive points

On Game Exchange page you will recive requests for your games, after you approve game exchange you will meet with member, exchange game and recive your points.

Play Games

With points in your balance go to our library and search for games you would like to play, send a borrow request and enjoy playing games.

Enjoy GamedIn Community

Rate and review games you played, earn points in a lot of ways, reffer friends, eearn badges, expect surprises and gifts!
